Banana Bread

  1. Ingredients:
  2. 1 cup granulated sugar
  3. 8 tablespoons/1 stick of unsalted butter
  4. 2 large eggs
  5. 3 bananas rippened
  6. 1 tablespoon of milk
  7. 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  8. 2 cups of allpurpose flour
  9. 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  10. 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  11. 1 teaspoon salt

  1. Directions:
  2. 1. preheat oven to 325 degrees F. Butter up a loaf pan
  3. 2. Cream the sugar and butter in large bowl until it's light and fluffy.
  4. 3. Mash bananas in another bowl then mix in milk and cinnamon.
  5. 4.In another bowl mix the flour, baking powder and baking soda with the salt.
  6. 5. Pour the batter into the loaf pan then bake for 1 hour and 10 min.
  7. 6. Let it cool for about 15 minutes
  8. 7. Enjoy

Twice Baked Potato

  1. Ingredients:
  2. 8 potatoes, washed
  3. 3 tablespoons of oil,canola prefered
  4. 2 sticks of salted butter
  5. 1 cup of bacon bits
  6. 1 cup sour cream
  7. 1 cup of cheedar cheese
  8. 1 cup whole milk
  9. 2 teaspons seasoned salt
  10. 3 sliced green onions
  11. ground black pepper

  1. Directions:
  2. 1. preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  3. 2. place potatos on a backing sheet and rub them with the oil. Bake for 1 hour
  4. 3. Slice butter into pats. Place in a large bowl and add the bacon and sour cream
  5. 4. Remove potatoes from oven and lower the heat to 350 degrees F.
  6. 5. Cut each potato in half and scoop the insides into the mixing bowl. Leave some potato around the rim for support.
    Lay the hollowed out shell on a baking sheet
  7. 6. Smash the potatoes into the butter, bacon, and sour cream. Then add your cheese, milk, salt, onions, and pepper and mix well.
  8. 7. Fill the potato shells with filling then add a little more cheese on top, place them in the over for 15-20 minutes, then let cool and enjoy!
